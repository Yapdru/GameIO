🚀 GET STARTED IN 3 STEPS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. OPEN IN BROWSER
   ───────────────
   Option A: GitHub
     https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Yapdru/GameIO/claude/lucid-einstein-7xUfB/gameio-max-ult.html

   Option B: Local File
     Open gameio-max-ult.html in your web browser

   Option C: Local Server (Recommended)
     python3 -m http.server 8080
     Visit: http://localhost:8080/gameio-max-ult.html

2. CREATE YOUR PROFILE
   ──────────────────
   • Click "PRESS START" button
   • Select your avatar (8 emoji options)
   • Choose your car (6 emoji options)
   • Enter your player name (up to 20 characters)
   • Click "START ADVENTURE"

3. PLAY GAMES
   ──────────
   • Wait for the startup animation (Kenny G music plays)
   • See the lobby with 9 games
   • Click a game to play
   • Press ESC to return to lobby

📞 FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Can I play with my friends?
A: Yes! Create a server (C key) and share the room code, or use Quick Join (J).

Q: Does it need internet?
A: No, it works completely offline once loaded.

Q: Can I save my scores?
A: Currently demos only (data resets on refresh). Firebase integration planned.

Q: What happens if I close the browser?
A: All data resets. Future version will persist to LocalStorage.

Q: Can I customize the games?
A: Source code is in gameio-max-ult.html - feel free to modify!

Q: Will there be more games?
A: Space Race and others are planned for future versions.
